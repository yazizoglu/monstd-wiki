# Twin Dragon

![](/images/twin_dragon.png)

The Twin Dragon is the final boss of the [[Level 1|Cursed Wilds]] — a two-headed fire dragon with the highest HP of any Map 1 enemy. It is immune to Fire damage and moves slowly, but reaching wave 25 with full HP means an almost certain escape if the defense is under-built.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 3800 |
| Speed | Very Slow (30) |
| Armor | 55% Physical reduction |
| Resistances | Fire (immune), Physical (very heavy reduction) |
| Kill Reward | 200 gold |
| Appears | Wave 25 — Cursed Wilds (Final Boss) |

---

## Traits

- **Fire Immune** — Immune to Fire damage. Frost deals 50% bonus damage.
- **Armor Plated (heavy)** — 55% physical reduction. Lightning deals bonus damage.

---

## Strategy

Twin Dragon demands Frost and Lightning. Physical towers (Cannon) are badly hampered by 55% armor. Poison has no resistance modifier — it is the secondary damage source. The key counter is Frost Tower: the dragon is slow and ice-weak, so stacking Frost upgrades extending the slow duration significantly increases total DPS time.

**Counters:** [[Frost Tower]] (ice bonus + slow), [[Lightning Tower]] (armor bonus), [[Poison Tower]] (no resistance)

**Avoid:** Fire-based upgrades — immune. [[Cannon Tower]] — 55% armor makes splash damage minimal.
