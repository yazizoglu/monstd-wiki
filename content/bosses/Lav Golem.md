# Lav Golem

The Lav Golem is the first boss of the [[Level 2|Volcanic Inferno]] — a molten rock construct that is completely immune to Fire damage. Players who arrived from Map 1 relying on fire-upgraded towers will find this boss impossible to counter without repositioning their build.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 950 |
| Speed | Slow (35) |
| Armor | 35% Physical reduction |
| Resistances | Fire (immune) |
| Kill Reward | 42 gold |
| Appears | Wave 5 — Volcanic Inferno |

---

## Traits

- **Fire Immune** — Immune to Fire damage. Frost deals 50% bonus damage.
- **Armor Plated** — Physical reduction. Lightning deals bonus damage.

---

## Strategy

The Lav Golem is the game's first hard resist check. Players who used fire upgrades on towers must switch to Frost, Lightning, or Poison. Frost Tower is the ideal counter — it provides both the ice bonus damage and the slow.

**Counters:** [[Frost Tower]] (immune bonus + slow), [[Lightning Tower]] (armor bonus), [[Poison Tower]]

**Avoid:** Any fire damage upgrade paths — completely ineffective.
