# Lizardman

![](/images/lizardman.png)

The Lizardman is the penultimate boss of the [[Level 2|Volcanic Inferno]] — a large reptilian warrior at 3300 HP with substantial armor. It has no elemental immunities, making tower choice flexible, but its HP demands a maxed-out economy by wave 20.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 3300 |
| Speed | Slow (45) |
| Armor | 38% Physical reduction |
| Resistances | None |
| Kill Reward | 102 gold |
| Appears | Wave 20 — Volcanic Inferno |

---

## Traits

- **Armor Plated (heavy)** — 38% Physical reduction. Lightning deals bonus damage.

---

## Strategy

The Lizardman is a raw DPS gate at wave 20. Heavy armor makes Fire Tower less efficient. Lightning Tower is preferred for the armor bonus. Poison DoT and Frost slow extend the damage window on a slow-moving target.

**Counters:** [[Lightning Tower]] (armor bonus), [[Poison Tower]] (DoT at scale), [[Frost Tower]] (slow)
