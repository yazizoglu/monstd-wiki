# Ice Golem

![](/images/ice_golem.png)

The Ice Golem is the first boss of the [[Level 3|Frozen Tundra]] — a towering construct of enchanted ice immune to Frost damage. It hits hard against defenses built around Frost slow mechanics.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 1100 |
| Speed | Slow (32) |
| Armor | 40% Physical reduction |
| Resistances | Frost (immune) |
| Kill Reward | 38 gold |
| Appears | Wave 5 — Frozen Tundra |

---

## Traits

- **Frost Immune** — Immune to Frost damage. Fire deals 50% bonus damage.
- **Armor Plated** — 40% Physical reduction. Lightning deals bonus damage.

---

## Strategy

Frost Tower players coming from Map 1 will find their main slow and damage source completely useless here. Fire-upgraded towers and Lightning Tower are the counters. The Ice Golem is slow enough that time is not the constraint — damage type selection is.

**Counters:** [[Lightning Tower]] (armor bonus), fire damage upgrades (ice weakness), [[Poison Tower]]

**Avoid:** [[Frost Tower]] — immune.
