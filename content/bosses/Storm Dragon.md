# Storm Dragon

## Overview

The Storm Dragon is the final boss of [[Level 3]] and the most dangerous enemy in the game. A flying lightning elemental, it moves erratically along the path and emits a chain lightning aura that damages nearby towers' effectiveness. Its frost resistance and flight combine to neutralize two of the most reliable tower types.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Appears | [[Level 3]], Wave 25 |
| Damage Type | Lightning (aura) |
| Resistances | Frost, Physical |
| Life Penalty | 10 lives |
| Wave Bonus | Cancelled on escape |

---

## Core Mechanic

**Erratic Flight + Lightning Aura.** The Storm Dragon does not follow the path predictably — it surges forward and occasionally loops back, making timing-based strategies unreliable. Its lightning aura interrupts fire rate on nearby towers.

---

## Phases

| Phase | Trigger | Behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | 100–70% HP | Standard path with occasional surges |
| Phase 2 | 70–40% HP | More frequent surges, aura radius increases |
| Phase 3 | Below 40% HP | Continuous surges, maximum aura disruption |

---

## Weaknesses

- [[Poison]] DoT — continues dealing damage between aura pulses
- [[Fire]] ([[Cannon Tower]]) — no fire resistance on the Dragon
- High-range towers outside the aura radius

---

## Strong Against

- [[Frost Tower]] — frost resistance eliminates value entirely
- [[Arrow Tower]] / [[Cannon Tower]] — physical resistance reduces damage
- Close-range towers — caught in the lightning aura disruption zone

---

## Recommended Towers

- [[Poison Tower]] — DoT is the most reliable sustained damage source
- [[Cannon Tower]] — fire-typed splash (no fire resistance); handles minion waves
- [[Lightning Tower]] — resisted but chain can still find value on escorting enemies
- [[Arrow Tower]] — long range keeps towers outside the aura disruption zone
