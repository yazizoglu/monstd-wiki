# Vampire

![](../images/vampire.png)

The Vampire appears as the wave 15 boss on both the [[Level 2|Volcanic Inferno]] and [[Level 3|Frozen Tundra]]. A blood-magic predator with powerful regeneration and immunity to Frost, it is the first regenerating boss in the game and demands Poison coverage to counter.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 3000 |
| Speed | Medium (50) |
| Armor | 25% Physical reduction |
| Resistances | Frost (immune) |
| Kill Reward | 88 gold |
| Appears | Wave 15 — Volcanic Inferno & Frozen Tundra |

---

## Traits

- **Regenerating** — Heals 2% of max HP per second. Poison deals 50% bonus damage.
- **Frost Immune** — Immune to Frost damage. Fire deals 50% bonus damage.

---

## Strategy

The Vampire's regeneration means sustained low-DPS fire lets it heal faster than it takes damage. Poison Tower is the mandatory counter — it deals 50% bonus damage to regenerating enemies and bypasses regeneration with DoT. Frost Tower is completely useless (immune). Fire damage from upgrades deals bonus damage.

At 3000 HP with regeneration, missing the Poison counter can make this boss unkillable.

**Counters:** [[Poison Tower]] (mandatory — 50% bonus vs regenerating), [[Arrow Tower]] (fire rate prevents healing), fire-upgraded towers

**Avoid:** [[Frost Tower]] — immune.
