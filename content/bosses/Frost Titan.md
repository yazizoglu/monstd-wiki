# Frost Titan

## Overview

The Frost Titan is the final boss of [[Level 2]]. A colossal armored giant encased in ice, he combines heavy physical armor with frost aura that temporarily slows nearby towers. He requires a build that can deal meaningful damage while its own towers are periodically disabled.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Appears | [[Level 2]], Wave 25 |
| Damage Type | Frost (aura) |
| Resistances | Frost, Physical |
| Life Penalty | 10 lives |
| Wave Bonus | Cancelled on escape |

---

## Core Mechanic

**Tower Freeze Aura.** When the Frost Titan enters a tower's range, it emits a brief freeze pulse that disables towers in the immediate area for a short duration. Players must spread towers across the path to avoid mass disabling.

---

## Phases

| Phase | Trigger | Behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | 100–50% HP | Steady advance, occasional freeze pulse |
| Phase 2 | Below 50% HP | Continuous freeze aura active, armor value increases |

---

## Weaknesses

- [[Lightning]] damage — bypasses physical armor
- [[Poison]] DoT — frost/physical resistance doesn't block DoT
- [[Fire]] ([[Cannon Tower]]) — fire resistance not present; splash helps vs armor

---

## Strong Against

- [[Frost Tower]] — frost resistance cancels both damage and slow
- [[Arrow Tower]] / [[Cannon Tower]] — physical armor drastically reduces damage
- Clustered tower placements — freeze aura disables multiple towers at once

---

## Recommended Towers

- [[Lightning Tower]] — mandatory; only reliable armor-piercing damage source
- [[Poison Tower]] — DoT continues during freeze aura downtime
- [[Arrow Tower]] — supplement at extreme range, outside freeze radius
