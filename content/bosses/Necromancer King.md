# Necromancer King

## Overview

The Necromancer King is the final boss of [[Level 1]]. A dark sorcerer mounted on an undead throne, he commands waves of skeletal minions and has strong frost resistance — a direct callback to the skeleton enemies encountered throughout the level.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Appears | [[Level 1]], Wave 25 |
| Damage Type | Arcane (on escape) |
| Resistances | Frost |
| Life Penalty | 10 lives |
| Wave Bonus | Cancelled on escape |

---

## Core Mechanic

**Minion Summoning.** The Necromancer King periodically summons [[Skeleton]] minions that travel alongside him. These minions must be cleared or they will compound the escape threat by overwhelming the final towers.

---

## Phases

| Phase | Trigger | Behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | 100–60% HP | Steady advance, moderate minion rate |
| Phase 2 | 60–30% HP | Increased minion spawn rate, slight speed boost |
| Phase 3 | Below 30% HP | Maximum minion rate, boss accelerates significantly |

---

## Weaknesses

- [[Lightning]] damage — no resistance penalty
- [[Poison]] DoT — effective throughout all phases
- [[Physical]] (Arrow) — reliable damage at range

---

## Strong Against

- [[Frost Tower]] — frost resistance reduces damage and slow effect
- Low fire-rate towers — boss moves faster in Phase 3

---

## Recommended Towers

- [[Lightning Tower]] — primary damage; also clears skeleton minions
- [[Arrow Tower]] — consistent ranged output on the boss
- [[Poison Tower]] — DoT deals sustained damage across all phases
- [[Cannon Tower]] — clears the skeleton minions efficiently
