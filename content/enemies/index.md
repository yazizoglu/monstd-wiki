# Enemies

Enemies travel along fixed paths toward your base. Each enemy type has unique stats, resistances, and behaviors designed to pressure specific tower builds.

---

## Enemy Design Goals

- Each enemy should exert pressure on a different defensive strategy
- Resistances force players to diversify their tower builds
- Speed, health, and resistances are the three primary threat axes
- Flying enemies require [[Arrow Tower]] or [[Lightning Tower]] to reliably counter
