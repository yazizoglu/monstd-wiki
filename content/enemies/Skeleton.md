# Skeleton

## Overview

Skeletons are mid-tier ground enemies with frost resistance and moderate health. They are a direct counter to frost-heavy builds and introduce the importance of damage diversification.

| Stat | Rating |
|---|---|
| Health | Medium |
| Speed | Medium |
| Armor | None |
| Resistances | Frost |
| Threat Level | Medium |

---

## Purpose

Skeletons punish players who over-invested in [[Frost Tower]]. Their frost resistance forces damage diversification and teaches the resistance system. They also introduce the [[Necromancer King]] thematic — the boss summons skeletal minions.

---

## Strengths

- Frost resistance reduces [[Frost Tower]] damage and slow effectiveness
- Medium speed means they don't linger long enough for DoT to fully resolve
- Appear in sufficient numbers to overwhelm single-target defenses

---

## Weaknesses

- No armor — [[Physical]], [[Lightning]], [[Poison]], and [[Fire]] deal full damage
- Moderate health means they are not tanky under focused fire
- Cannot fly

---

## Recommended Counters

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Lightning Tower]] | No resistance penalty; chain hits groups |
| [[Poison Tower]] | Full DoT damage; efficient on grouped skeletons |
| [[Arrow Tower]] | Consistent output; no resistance penalty |

---

## Appears In

- [[Level 1]] — introduced in later waves alongside [[Necromancer King]] thematic
- [[Level 2]] — appears in mixed resistance waves
