# Frost Plant

![](../images/plant.png)

## Overview

Frost Plants are slow, tanky creatures that appear only in the early waves of Frozen Tundra campaign levels. Their heavy shell makes Poison damage nearly useless against them.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 140 |
| Speed | Very Slow (38) |
| Armor | Moderate (15%) |
| Resistances | Poison (immune) |
| Kill Reward | 10 gold |

---

## Traits

- **Poison Resist** — Immune to Poison damage (75% reduction).

---

## Purpose

Frost Plants appear exclusively in waves 1–7 of Frozen Tundra campaign levels as early HP sponges that punish over-reliance on Poison Tower in the opening waves. Their slow speed gives players time to deal with them, but their Poison immunity forces build diversity from the start.

---

## Strengths

- Immune to Poison — a popular early-game tower is ineffective
- Moderate armor further reduces physical damage
- High HP for an early-wave enemy

---

## Weaknesses

- Extremely slow — towers have many shots to land
- Lightning deals bonus damage through armor
- Frost slow barely matters at this speed
