# Troll

![](/images/troll.png)

## Overview

Trolls are heavy ground units that soak up damage and push steadily through defenses. Slower than [[Goblin|Goblins]], they are significantly harder to kill and begin appearing from wave 4.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Base HP | 200 |
| Speed | Slow |
| Armor | 25% Physical reduction |
| Resistances | None |
| Kill Reward | 12 gold |

---

## Traits

- **Armor Plated** — Physical damage reduced by 35%. Lightning deals 35% bonus damage.

---

## Purpose

Trolls shift the threat from speed to durability. Early towers tuned for goblin swarms must also handle Troll HP — single-target towers become more valuable when Trolls are on the field.

---

## Strengths

- High health for a standard enemy — survives several tower hits
- Appear in waves well before any tank-busting towers are likely built
- Force players to invest in damage output, not just coverage

---

## Weaknesses

- Slow movement speed gives towers more time to fire
- No elemental resistances — Poison and Frost are fully effective
- Lightning Tower deals bonus damage due to Armor Plated weakness

---

## Recommended Counters

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Lightning Tower]] | Bonus damage against armor-plated enemies |
| [[Poison Tower]] | DoT damage ignores armor; effective on tanky enemies |
