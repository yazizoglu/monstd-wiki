# Golem

## Overview

The Golem is a heavily armored, high-health ground enemy. It moves slowly but absorbs enormous amounts of physical damage. Golems directly counter [[Physical]]-heavy builds and require targeted elemental responses.

| Stat | Rating |
|---|---|
| Health | Very High |
| Speed | Low |
| Armor | Heavy |
| Resistances | Physical, Fire |
| Threat Level | High |

---

## Purpose

Golems are damage sponges designed to stress-test the player's elemental diversity. A defense relying entirely on [[Arrow Tower]] and [[Cannon Tower]] will fail to eliminate Golems before they exit. They force investment in [[Lightning Tower]] or [[Poison Tower]].

---

## Strengths

- Heavy armor significantly reduces [[Physical]] damage
- Fire resistance reduces [[Frost Tower]] (fire-counter) effectiveness
- Very high health pool absorbs sustained damage
- Slow speed means they survive long enough to trigger multiple wave interactions

---

## Weaknesses

- Very slow — spends maximum time in tower range
- [[Lightning]] damage fully bypasses armor — direct hard counter
- [[Poison]] DoT deals full damage over time
- No flying capability

---

## Recommended Counters

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Lightning Tower]] | Armor-piercing; single best counter to Golems |
| [[Poison Tower]] | Full DoT damage regardless of armor |
| [[Frost Tower]] | Slow extends Golem's already-long time in range |
