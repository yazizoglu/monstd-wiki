# Goblin

![](/images/goblin.png)

## Overview

Goblins are the most common enemy in MonsTD. They are fast, low-health, and appear in large numbers. Individually weak, they are dangerous in swarms — especially early when towers are sparse.

| Stat | Rating |
|---|---|
| Health | Low |
| Speed | High |
| Armor | None |
| Resistances | None |
| Threat Level | Low (high in swarms) |

---

## Purpose

Goblins test the player's area-damage coverage. A defense built entirely from single-target towers will struggle against goblin swarm waves. They also serve as a gold-denial threat — each goblin that escapes costs a life.

---

## Strengths

- High movement speed makes them hard to eliminate before they reach the exit
- Large group size overwhelms single-target towers
- Appear early before the player has many towers placed

---

## Weaknesses

- Extremely low health — any area tower kills them efficiently
- No resistances — all damage types are fully effective
- Cannot fly
