# Wolf

## Overview

Wolves are fast, medium-health ground enemies that apply sustained pressure through speed. They are harder to kill than [[Goblin|Goblins]] and move too quickly to handle with slow-firing towers alone.

| Stat | Rating |
|---|---|
| Health | Medium |
| Speed | Very High |
| Armor | None |
| Resistances | None |
| Threat Level | Medium |

---

## Purpose

Wolves test fire rate and target prioritization. They can slip through gaps in coverage that Goblins cannot, punishing poorly placed or under-upgraded towers. They introduce the importance of fire rate as a tower stat.

---

## Strengths

- Fastest movement speed among standard ground enemies
- Enough health to survive light tower coverage
- Often appear in groups, compounding the speed pressure

---

## Weaknesses

- No armor or resistances — all damage types deal full damage
- Medium health means two or three tower hits will eliminate them
- Cannot fly

---

## Recommended Counters

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Arrow Tower]] | High fire rate handles individual wolves efficiently |
| [[Lightning Tower]] | Chain hits multiple wolves in quick succession |
| [[Frost Tower]] | Slow reduces effective speed, giving other towers time to fire |
