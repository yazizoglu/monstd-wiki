# Flying Bat

## Overview

Flying Bats are airborne enemies that travel at medium speed along the path. They cannot be hit by [[Cannon Tower]] splash and are only reliably targeted by [[Arrow Tower]] and [[Lightning Tower]]. Their main threat is forcing the player to maintain anti-air coverage at all times.

| Stat | Rating |
|---|---|
| Health | Low–Medium |
| Speed | Medium |
| Armor | None |
| Resistances | None |
| Threat Level | Medium |

---

## Purpose

Flying Bats introduce the flying enemy mechanic. Players who neglect [[Arrow Tower]] in favour of pure area-damage builds will find Bats slipping through uncontested. They punish over-specialization.

---

## Strengths

- Cannot be targeted by [[Cannon Tower]] splash
- [[Frost Tower]] has reduced effectiveness (ground-targeted slow less effective vs flyers)
- Forces dedicated anti-air tower coverage
- Often appear in mixed waves alongside ground enemies, dividing attention

---

## Weaknesses

- Low health — destroyed quickly by [[Arrow Tower]] or [[Lightning Tower]]
- No resistances — all elemental damage types deal full damage
- Medium speed means they are catchable with any functional anti-air setup

---

## Recommended Counters

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Arrow Tower]] | Primary anti-air; high range reaches flyers reliably |
| [[Lightning Tower]] | Chain can jump to Flying Bats near ground clusters |

---

## Appears In

- [[Level 2]] — introduced
- [[Level 3]] — mixed with frost-resistant ground enemies
