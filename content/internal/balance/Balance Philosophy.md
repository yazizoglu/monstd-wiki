# Balance Philosophy

Core principles that guide all balance decisions in MonsTD. When a specific number is unclear, refer back to these principles.

---

## 1. No Dominant Strategy

No single tower or combination should be clearly optimal on all levels. A player who builds identically every run should hit a wall by Level 2.

**In practice:**
- Every tower should have at least one level where it is sub-optimal
- Every tower should have at least one level where it is the best pick
- Resistances are the primary mechanism that enforces this

---

## 2. Readable Counterplay

Players should be able to understand why they are losing and what to change. Damage resistances, special mechanics, and boss phases must be communicated in the UI.

**In practice:**
- Enemy resist icons visible in the inspection panel
- Boss phase transitions should be telegraphed visually
- Damage matchups summarized in [[Damage Types Guide]]

---

## 3. Difficulty Through Complexity, Not Attrition

Hard difficulty should add strategic complexity, not just HP multipliers. Harder versions of enemies should have more resistances or mechanics, not just 3× health.

**In practice:**
- Normal difficulty: teaches mechanics cleanly
- Hard difficulty: adds resistance variants, faster waves, tighter economy
- HP scaling caps at +50% on Hard to avoid pure attrition gameplay

---

## 4. Economy Rewards Skill, Not Time

Gold income should scale with smart play — completing waves faster, using fewer lives — not with passive waiting. Bonus gold from wave completion should be the primary income above base kill gold.

**In practice:**
- Wave bonus cancellation on final boss escape is a meaningful loss condition
- Selling and rebuilding has an inherent gold cost (60% sell value)
- Crystal meta-upgrades give early-game advantages, not late-game stat inflation

---

## 5. Boss Fights Are Dramatic Events

Every boss should feel dangerous. A boss that reaches the exit should always feel like a crisis, not routine. The 10-life penalty for final bosses is intentional — it forces the player to view the final wave as the most important.

---

## 6. Build Diversity Over Optimal Builds

The meta should shift between levels. A player who "solved" Level 1 with Arrow + Cannon + Frost should need to fundamentally rethink for Level 3. This is achieved through resistance layering, not arbitrary enemy health scaling.
