# Frost

## Overview

Frost damage slows enemies on hit and deals significant bonus damage against fire-resistant enemies (+85%). The slow component makes Frost valuable as support even when the damage bonus does not apply, and the matchup bonus makes it the definitive counter to fire-immune bosses like [[Lav Golem]].

---

## Towers

- [[Frost Tower]] — single target, slows on hit

---

## Matchup Table

| vs Enemy | Multiplier | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Frost-resistant | ×0.25 (−75%) | Nearly useless — switch element |
| Fire-resistant | **×1.85 (+85%)** | Primary counter — bonus + slow combo |
| No resistance | ×1.0 + slow | Slow is always useful |

---

## Design Notes

- The slow effect is the primary value of Frost — damage is secondary in neutral matchups
- Against fire-resistant enemies, Frost provides both the best damage bonus and the slow — making it uniquely powerful there
- Frost resistance should appear often enough that players cannot auto-include [[Frost Tower]] in every build
- [[Level 3]] (Frozen Tundra) has high frost resistance — Frost Tower loses value there intentionally
