# Frost

## Overview

Frost damage slows enemies on hit and deals moderate damage. It is strong against fire-resistant enemies and weak against frost-resistant ones. The slow component makes Frost highly valuable as support even when the damage bonus does not apply.

---

## Towers

- [[Frost Tower]] — single target, slows on hit

---

## Matchup Table

| vs Enemy | Effectiveness |
|---|---|
| Frost-resistant | Reduced damage + reduced slow |
| Fire-resistant | **Bonus damage** |
| Armored | Standard |
| Poison-resistant | Standard |
| No resistance | Standard + slow |

---

## Design Notes

- The slow effect is the primary value of Frost — damage is secondary
- Frost resistance should appear often enough that players cannot auto-include [[Frost Tower]] in every build
- [[Level 3]] (Frozen Tundra) intentionally has high frost resistance among enemies — Frost Tower loses value there
