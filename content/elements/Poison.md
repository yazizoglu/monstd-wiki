# Poison

## Overview

Poison applies a damage-over-time (DoT) effect that continues after the projectile lands. Against regenerating enemies it deals **double damage** (+100%), making it the most decisive elemental counter in the game. With [[Gnoll]] now the dominant ground enemy of the Volcanic Inferno, Poison Tower becomes a core build choice in those maps.

---

## Towers

- [[Poison Tower]] — single target, applies DoT on hit

---

## Matchup Table

| vs Enemy | Multiplier | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Poison-resistant | ×0.25 (−75%) | Avoid — negligible output |
| Regenerating | **×2.0 (+100%)** | Double damage — hard counter |
| No resistance | ×1.0 DoT | Still useful on long paths |

---

## Design Notes

- ×2.0 against regenerating enemies is the highest single-matchup multiplier in the game — Poison Tower earns its slot in any wave with Gnolls
- DoT does not stack from multiple Poison Towers — only the strongest active tick applies
- The "anti-regen" role is communicated in the damage number display — watch for the green ▲N on Gnoll hits
- Poison has no second strong matchup (unlike Fire/Frost which counter each other) — broader utility at slightly lower peak
