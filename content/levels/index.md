# Levels

MonsTD features three campaign worlds, each with distinct terrain, enemy compositions, and boss encounters. Each level consists of 25 waves. Boss waves occur at waves 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25.

---

## Campaign Worlds

| Level | World | Theme | Final Boss |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[Level 1]] | Cursed Wilds | Ground enemies, escalating armor | [[Twin Dragon]] |
| [[Level 2]] | Volcanic Inferno | Flying enemies, fire immunity | [[Demon]] |
| [[Level 3]] | Frozen Tundra | Frost immunity, heavy armor | [[Frostmourne Demon]] |

---

## Difficulty System

Each level can be played on **Easy**, **Normal**, or **Hard**. Harder difficulties increase enemy health, speed, and resistance values, and unlock separate leaderboard entries.

---

## Wave Structure

- Waves 1–4: Standard enemies, increasing pressure
- Wave 5: Mini-boss
- Waves 6–9: Harder standard mix
- Wave 10: Mini-boss
- … (repeating pattern)
- Wave 25: **Final Boss** — escape costs 10 lives and cancels wave bonus
