# Towers

Towers are the core defensive units in MonsTD. Each tower has a distinct damage type, role, and upgrade path. Effective play requires mixing tower types to cover elemental weaknesses.

---

## Tower Roster

| Tower | Damage Type | Role | Special |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[Arrow Tower]] | [[Physical]] | Generalist, long range | Strong vs flying |
| [[Cannon Tower]] | [[Physical]] | Area denial | Splash damage |
| [[Frost Tower]] | [[Frost]] | Crowd control | Slows enemies |
| [[Lightning Tower]] | [[Lightning]] | Chain damage | Hits multiple targets |
| [[Poison Tower]] | [[Poison]] | Sustained damage | Damage over time |

---

## Design Principles

- Every tower has a clear role — no tower should do everything well
- Damage types create a matchup layer that rewards build planning
- Tower placement and synergy matter as much as tower choice
- See [[elements/index|Elements]] for full resistance and matchup rules
