# Towers

Towers are the core defensive units in MonsTD. Each tower has a distinct damage type, role, and upgrade path. Effective play requires mixing tower types to cover elemental weaknesses.

---

## Tower Roster

| Tower | Damage Type | Role | Base Cost | Special |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [[Arrow Tower]] | [[Physical]] | Generalist, long range | 60g | Targets flying; highest range |
| [[Cannon Tower]] | [[Physical]] | Area denial | 90g | Splash damage on impact |
| [[Frost Tower]] | [[Frost]] | Crowd control | 80g | Slows all hit enemies |
| [[Lightning Tower]] | [[Lightning]] | Chain / anti-armor | 120g | Bolt chains to nearby targets |
| [[Poison Tower]] | [[Poison]] | Sustained damage | 100g | Damage over time |

---

## Synergy Overview

| Pair | Why It Works |
|---|---|
| [[Frost Tower]] + [[Cannon Tower]] | Frost clusters slow enemies; Cannon splash hits all of them |
| [[Frost Tower]] + [[Poison Tower]] | Slow extends DoT duration — maximum damage per enemy |
| [[Arrow Tower]] + [[Lightning Tower]] | Arrow covers flying; Lightning chains armored ground enemies |
| [[Lightning Tower]] + [[Frost Tower]] | Frost clusters enemies for chain jumps |

---

## When to Build Each Tower

- **[[Arrow Tower]]** — place first on every map; the only anti-air alongside Lightning
- **[[Cannon Tower]]** — add when goblin swarm waves appear; pair with Frost
- **[[Frost Tower]]** — add before wave 5 boss; upstream of your main kill zone
- **[[Lightning Tower]]** — required from wave 8+ when armored enemies appear
- **[[Poison Tower]]** — mandatory on levels with regenerating bosses ([[Vampire]])

---

## Design Principles

- Every tower has a clear role — no tower should do everything well
- Damage types create a matchup layer that rewards build planning
- Tower placement and synergy matter as much as tower choice
