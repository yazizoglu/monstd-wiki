# Lightning Tower

![](/images/tower_lightning.png)

## Overview

The Lightning Tower fires bolts that chain between nearby enemies, hitting multiple targets per shot. It is the primary counter to armored enemies and excels in dense waves where chain jumps can clear multiple targets simultaneously.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage Type | [[Lightning]] |
| Target | Chain (multi-target) |
| Range | Medium |
| Fire Rate | Medium |
| Special | Bolt chains to nearby enemies |

---

## Role

**Chain Damage / Anti-Armor.** Lightning bypasses physical armor, making it the hard counter to armored enemies like [[Troll]], [[Orc]], and boss [[Golem]]. The chain mechanic rewards dense wave placement.

---

## Strengths

- [[Lightning]] damage ignores armor — full damage vs armored enemies
- Chain hit multiplies damage output on grouped enemies
- Consistent damage pressure when enemies travel close together
- Strong pick on [[Level 2]] and [[Level 3]] where armored enemies appear frequently

---

## Weaknesses

- Frost-resistant enemies reduce Lightning damage
- Chain cannot hit flying enemies that are not near ground targets
- Highest cost of the core tower lineup
- Underperforms against spread-out or single-file enemy formations

---

## Synergies

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Frost Tower]] | Slowed enemies cluster → more chain jumps per bolt |
| [[Arrow Tower]] | Arrow handles flyers and long-range; Lightning handles armored clusters |
| [[Fire Tower]] | Fire Tower clears light enemy clusters; Lightning cleans up armored stragglers |

---

## Countered By

- Frost-resistant enemies — [[Lightning]] damage reduced
- Spread formations — chain has no valid targets to jump to

---

## Recommended Usage

- Essential pick whenever armored enemies ([[Troll]], [[Orc]], [[Golem]] boss) appear in wave composition
- Place at the densest part of the path for maximum chain value
- Combine with [[Frost Tower]] upstream for clustered chain opportunities
