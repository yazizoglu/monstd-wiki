# Cannon Tower

## Overview

The Cannon Tower fires explosive shells that deal area-of-effect damage on impact. It excels at destroying tightly packed enemy groups but has a lower fire rate and shorter range than the [[Arrow Tower]].

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage Type | [[Physical]] |
| Target | Area (Splash) |
| Range | Medium |
| Fire Rate | Low |
| Special | Splash damage on impact |

---

## Role

**Area Denial / Swarm Clearer.** The Cannon Tower punishes clustered enemies. It is the primary answer to fast, low-health swarm waves.

---

## Strengths

- Highest area damage output per shot
- Excellent against groups of light enemies ([[Goblin]], [[Wolf]])
- Splash damage hits targets behind the main target
- High burst potential on slow waves

---

## Weaknesses

- Low fire rate means it struggles against single fast targets
- Splash has no effect vs spread-out or flying enemies
- [[Physical]] damage is reduced by armored enemies ([[Golem]])
- Shorter range than [[Arrow Tower]]

---

## Synergies

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Arrow Tower]] | Arrow covers flyers and single targets Cannon can't reach |
| [[Frost Tower]] | Slowed enemies cluster tightly, maximising splash hits |
| [[Poison Tower]] | Poison softens grouped enemies for Cannon's burst |

---

## Countered By

- Armored enemies — [[Physical]] damage penalty applies
- Spread-out waves — splash value drops when enemies are spaced
- Fast single targets — low fire rate misses frequently

---

## Recommended Usage

- Place at tight bends or chokepoints where enemies cluster naturally
- Pair with [[Frost Tower]] to force clustering via slow
- Avoid relying on Cannon alone against boss waves — supplement with crowd control
