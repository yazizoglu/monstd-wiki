# Arrow Tower

![](/images/tower_arrow.png)

## Overview

The Arrow Tower is the foundational defensive unit in MonsTD. It fires single-target projectiles with high range and consistent output. Available from the start, it serves as the backbone of most early builds.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage Type | [[Physical]] |
| Target | Single |
| Range | High |
| Fire Rate | Medium |
| Special | Strong vs Flying |

---

## Role

**Generalist / Long-Range Sniper.** The Arrow Tower fills gaps in coverage and reliably deals with single high-priority targets. It is the best tower for targeting flying enemies.

---

## Strengths

- Longest base range of all towers
- Consistent, reliable damage output
- Only tower (alongside [[Lightning Tower]]) that can target [[Imp|flying enemies]]
- No elemental resistance penalty on most enemy types

---

## Weaknesses

- No area damage — weak against large swarms
- [[Physical]] damage is reduced by armored enemies ([[Troll]], [[Knight]], [[Golem]] boss)
- Below-average damage per shot compared to specialized towers

---

## Synergies

| Tower | Reason |
|---|---|
| [[Cannon Tower]] | Cannon handles swarms; Arrow handles priority targets and flyers |
| [[Frost Tower]] | Slowed enemies spend more time in Arrow's range |
| [[Lightning Tower]] | Lightning handles armored; Arrow handles the rest |

---

## Countered By

- Armored enemies ([[Troll]], [[Knight]], [[Golem]] boss) — Physical damage reduced
- Fast swarms — Arrow's single-target fire cannot keep up

---

## Recommended Usage

- Place early at high-traffic chokepoints
- Always station at least one on flyer-heavy levels ([[Level 2]], [[Level 3]])
- Prioritize range upgrades to extend coverage arcs
